What I'd Love to Hear About

This guide is a community resource and it gets better with community input. If you've spotted something wrong, found a mod that should be on the list, or have a suggestion for a new section, the form below goes straight to a spreadsheet I check regularly.

🐛
Corrections
Something on the guide is wrong or out of date - a folder path, a mod description, a broken link.
🧩
Missing Mods
A mod that should be on the must-have list but isn't. Include the mod name and where to find it.
💡
Suggestions
A new page, section, or topic you'd like to see covered on the guide.
🔗
Broken Links
A download link or external site link that's no longer working.
Responses go into a Google Sheet that I review when I update the guide. I may not reply directly, but I do read everything and useful suggestions will make it into future updates - credited if you'd like.

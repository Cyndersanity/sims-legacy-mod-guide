Archive Sites

Large collections from the original era - mostly still working

The Sims Resource - Sims 1 Free Account Required
Huge archive of clothing, hair and objects from the original game era. Some items need a free account to download.
↗ thesimsresource.com
Sims File Share - YoNonna
Massive collection of CC shared by Creator YoNonna. Recently collated and updated to be more accessible. Tonnes of build objects, hairs, skins and more. Thousands of files.
↗ simfileshare.net
Andrasta's World
Gothic and grungy style CC and mods. Unique dark aesthetic for atmospheric builds.
↗ doortomore.net
A Little White Magic
Gameplay plugins, decor and mods. Home of the Domcal Weekends mod.
↗ white.leadpipecollection.net
Sims Freaks / The Real Sim
Objects, clothes, houses and general build mode items.
↗ simfreaks.com
Wayback Machine - Sims 1 CC Listing
Large list of zip files containing loads of different CC options archived from the original era.
↗ archive.org
Wayback Machine - Sims 1 CC Search
Search the Wayback Machine to find archived Sims 1 CC sites from the original era.
↗ web.archive.org

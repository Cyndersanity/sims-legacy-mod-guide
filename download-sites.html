The current archive (mid-2009) also contains many old gift and contest items which were not previously made available on the Juniper Sun site.Apr-26

MistyRed – Sims Decor ↗Big range of themed sets, deco, objects, lots, CAS Skins/Heads etc.Apr-26
Moni's Sims ↗Huge range of objects, build mode, importable lots, CAS Skins/Heads/Meshes. Some really lovely walls and floors here. There are some sets based on Rivendell from LOTR and some cool animated curtians. There's also an entire range of downloads for you to build and furnish "The Simpsons" house!Apr-26
Nocturnal Market ↗Objects, gorgeous deco, themed sets and skins etc. Most recent download available is deco from Apr-26. I personally love the "Vampire the Masquerade" sets, as I played the heck out of this game.Apr-26
Nyx's Stuff ↗Huge range of objects, build mode and some CAS Heads/skins. Note: I downloaded quite a lot of Nyx's bedroom sets, only to realise later that majority of the beds are for deco only and not functional use. Some will have "Deco Bed" in the title when buying them in game. I ended up removing a lot of these as I had no use for non functional beds, but they do have big room scores.Apr-26
OddSim ↗Collection of cool buy mode objects, deco, build mode items and some CAS Skins. There's a range of ambient sounds that you can add into your game as well.Apr-26

Parsimonious: The Sims 1 ↗Massive collection of objects, sets, CAS Skins/Heads/Meshes, some really cool build mode items, hacked furniture etc. I use a good chunk of Parsimonious's downloads and love how crisp they look in game.Apr-26
Persimmon Grove ↗Large range of Objects, build mode (floors and walls), Lots and some themed sets. A good chunk are wooden/vintage looking.Apr-26
Secret Society of Woobsha ↗The holy grail of hacked objects, lots, themed sets, some seriously cool build items, unique apartment hack and tonnes of tutorials on how to create your own CC! Woobsha is a site I come back to often. A great starting point if you want to learn how to make CC. I've linked the tutorial section into my pages about creating your own CC and iff hacking.Apr-26
Shiny Things ↗Large range of lovely objects, heads, skins, walls, floors, and more.Apr-26
Simblr.cc – Sims 1 Mods ↗Gameplay hacks, bug fixes, new objects and tutorials.Apr-26
Sims-Lambda – ModTheSims ↗Modern buy mode and build mode objects created in 2025. Link is to the ModTheSims forum post and has more links to the rest of Sims-Lambda's downloads on SimsFileShare.Apr-26
Simlogical ↗Blog style site with range of hacked objects, lots, build mode objects (highight: lockable doors) and game controllers for visitor control and other mechanicesApr-26
SIMcredible! Designs ↗Lots of beautiful furniture sets for all rooms, outdoor patio sets and party sets.Apr-26
SimFreaks ↗Massive range of objects, build mode, skins, heads, meshes. Lots of sets to choose from. There's a range of good tutorials to follow here on creating CC as well.Apr-26
Simslice ↗Some seriously cool mods here! There are lots of objects and hacks, rideable motorbikes, a huge minigame mod called "Slice City" which allows you to place a small city object on your lawn for your sims to use. CAS Skins/Heads/Meshes, Pets etc. I recommend a deep-dive on this site.Apr-26
SomeSimThings ↗Large range of build and buy mode objects, some really cool hacked items, tutorials and some helpful objects replacements for the Superstar lots to help with the difficulty of certain fame interactions (Catwalk, Acting etc). There's some really neat body skill hacked objects too like fencing and yoga.Apr-26
Stephanie Too Neighbourhood ↗This site contains many beautiful Sims skins, some really useful hacks, objects, build mode (walls/floors etc) and some downloadable Lots.Apr-26
Stone Age Sims – The Next Evolution ↗Large range of Stone Age themed sets, objects, build mode items, skins etc. Note: The site is active, but at the time of checking I was unable to get any of the download sections to display properly for me. Have tried to reach out to Bjorn of Stone Age Sims to see if they're still maintaining the CC.Apr-26
The Real Sim ↗Huge range of buy mode objects, build mode items, lots and CAS Skins/Meshes/Heads. There are some deco cars you can download to give the illusion of owning vehicles/driving cars in the gameApr-26
The Sims Resource | Sims 1 - Downloads ↗Thousands upon thousands of downloads: Objects, sets, skins, build mode, lots and tonnes more. Note: The Sims Resource has been around for two decades. There's a massive range of stuff here, but it can be annoying getting the download links to work. There was a rumour recently that some creators had their accounts compromised and their content potentially corrupted with malware. I highly recommend you scan everything you download and use a VPN if possible. Github user Xientraa has created a neat little tool called "The Sims Resource Downloader" to help bypass some of the jankiness of the TSR downloading rules.Apr-26
The Sims Emporium ↗Nyx of Nyx's Stuff/Emporium runs this site. Massive range of build mode items, gorgeous objects/sets and lots of content created as gifts for Nyx by other creators.Apr-26
Vivacious ↗Contemporary, steampunk, and other themed Sims 1 stuff. There are Witcher themed skin sets, and the other CAS Skins/Heads/Meshes are gorgeous and incredibly well made.Apr-26
ZombieSims – Will Your Sims Survive? ↗Zombie Sims is a joint project from Simslice.com (Steve Alvey) & SimFreaks.com (Heather Castillo-Alvey). ZombieSims is a game add-on for The Sims 1 full game installations with all expansion packs. Gameplay is customized for the ZombieSims expansion pack. View the gallery for a detailed explanation of each objects functionality.Apr-26
Eves TS1 Recolors ↗Maxis base game objects re-coloured (build and buy mode mostly)Apr-26
The Sims Online Conversions ↗Almost complete collection of every TSO object, converted to work in the Sims 1. Raindrops has provided a database sheet to show where they got the content from, as well as a changelog.Apr-26

Archive Sites

Large collections from the original era and community-compiled archives

WebsiteContent TypeLast Verified Active
YoNonna – Sims File Share Directory ↗Directory containing a boat load of zipped together content that YoNonna has collated and saved on Sims File Share. Broken down into Creators/Website names with subfolders of all of the content YoNonna managed to track down and archive. Recently updated to be more accessible. Tonnes of build objects, hairs, skins and more – thousands of files, so you might want to check through the zips and choose what you want to keep. There are limitations to how many CC files can be used in the Sims game. If you go over the limit, the game may crash or not load.Apr-26
Wayback Machine – Sims 1 CC Listing ↗Large list of zip files containing loads of different CC options archived from the original Sims era.Apr-26
Mod The Sims – WCIF Sims 1 Mods/CC? ↗A large forum post containing lists of previously active Sims 1 CC sites, I haven't had time to go through it completely yet. But you may find other active sites that I haven't listed anywhere yet.Apr-26
Mod The Sims – TS1 - Maxis Beta Ports [1998] ↗Forum containing some of the beta objects that were shown in orignal Sims 1 Trailers, but never made it into the game.Apr-26
Saving the Sims Archive (s1) ↗Massive collection of content created between 2000 and now, put together by moderator Ginnie. Broken down into the website/creator with links to Sims File Share to download each set/zip file.Apr-26

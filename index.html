◆ Legacy Collection 2025 Updated March 2026 Windows 10 / 11
Welcome back to the neighbourhood! The Sims Legacy Collection launched in January 2025, and over a year later the modding community is well established and growing. Almost all classic Sims 1 mods (2000–2003) still work, new mods are being made specifically for Legacy, and this guide covers everything you need to get started.
